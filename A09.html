Andrew Ross Sorkin, Author of Too Big to Fail

November 2015

New York Times Columnmist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as „the most famous financial jouranlist of his generation. His book, Too Big Too Fail, was teh first behind-the-scenes account of the financial crisis that led to our current reccecsion.”

The Economist, The Financial Times, and Buisness Week, all named Too Big to Fail one of the best books of the year. The book was published by Viking October 20, 2009. The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premiered on HBO on May 23, 2011.
